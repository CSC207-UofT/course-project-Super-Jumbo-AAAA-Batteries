Class name: ListManager
'Role': Controller

Responsibilities:
* Calls corresponding use case method(s) based on input from driver
* Prints the inventory or shopping list
* Adds an item to inventory or shopping list
* Removes an item from inventory or shopping list
* Will include moving an item from shopping list to inventory and vice versa
* Saves inventory and shopping list for future use

Collaborators:
* InventoryUseCases
* ShoppingListUseCases
